Jennifer (Jen) Davis is Chief Technologist for the Model Based Systems Engineering Discipline Team in the Applied Research & Technology organization at Collins Aerospace. Dr. Davis has served as the Collins Aerospace principal investigator for several government-sponsored research programs on developing and applying high-assurance, model-based approaches to the verification and validation of autonomous systems. She chairs the Model Based Systems Engineering Working Group at Collins. Dr. Davis has worked for Collins Aerospace for fourteen years and has published seven papers. She holds a five patents and a Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Nebraska at Lincoln.
